# Emotion Regulation (Process Model)

## TheoryConcept
The Process Model of Emotion Regulation distinguishes between antecedent-focused strategies (occurring before an emotion is fully generated, e.g., cognitive reappraisal) and response-focused strategies (occurring after, e.g., expressive suppression).
